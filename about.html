Who is Sebastian Schally?

Sebastian Schally is an Austrian business executive and digital strategist who currently serves as the Managing Director and country lead of Accenture Song in Austria. He is recognized for his expertise in digital product development, public sector transformation, and AI-driven strategies.

Schally joined Accenture Song's leadership after the 2019 acquisition of the digital agency PXP/X, where he was formerly COO. His career spans roles as a software developer, Head of IT at Stepstone Österreich, and leadership positions in several digital and advertising firms. Schally has received multiple awards for digital innovation and has a background in media design and engineering, with extensive experience digitizing public and private sector services.
